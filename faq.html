Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a bid?

To place a bid, you need to create an account and log in. Once logged in, navigate to the item you want to bid on and enter your bid amount.

What happens if I win an auction?

If you win an auction, you will receive an email confirmation with payment and delivery details.

Are there any fees for bidding?

Bidding is free, but a buyer's premium may be added to the final price.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the 'Sign Up' button and filling in the required details.

Can I retract a bid?

No, once a bid is placed, it cannot be retracted. Please bid carefully.

How do I know if an item is authentic?

All items go through a verification process before being listed on the platform.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

How long does an auction last?

Can I sell my own items on the auction site?

Yes, registered users can list their items for auction after approval from our team.

Is there a customer support service?

Yes, we offer customer support via email and live chat. Visit our 'Contact Us' page for more details.

What happens if my item does not arrive?

If your item does not arrive within the estimated delivery time, please contact customer support for assistance.

Can I edit or delete my auction listing?

Yes, sellers can edit or delete their auction listings before any bids are placed. Once bidding has started, changes may be limited.
